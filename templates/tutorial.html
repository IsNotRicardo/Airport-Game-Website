Introduction

In this game you will spawn at a random airport, anywhere in the world. Your objective is to reach a specific airport in the minimum amount of attempts possible. An attempt is counted everytime you reach a different airport. Instructions on how to move can be found after starting a game.

Game Difficulty

There are three levels in game: Easy, Medium or Hard.

1. Easy: Only large airports.

2. Medium: Large and medium airports.

3. Hard: Large, medium and small airports.

Target Distances

Target Distances: Near, Average or Far.

1. Near: Between 1,000 and 5,000 kilometers.

2. Average: Between 5,000 and 11,000 kilometers.

3. Far: Above 11,000 kilometers.
